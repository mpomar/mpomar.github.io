About

I am one of those who believe everything happens for a reason. And most likely you ended up here because you would like to know more about me, either professionally or in my personal life.


According to my passport, I'm Italian but I am actually based in Germany, where I work for a large consulting company. I am a Salesforce professional with a major focus on integration & master data management topics. Additional information are available in my CV.


In my free time I take the chance to develop personal projects that have nothing to do with my job. You can find (some of) them in the Portfolio section. I am mainly into online/social media marketing and Python, which I use frequently for scraping and automation projects.

Portfolio
Lovescrape
Semi-automated webscraper

Ispired by this article and this TED Talk, I decided to use Selenium to solve a long unresolved problem: finding a partner. So I wrote this webscraper to get the user information from a large, German dating website. No, in the end it did not help me finding the right one... but it turned out to be a lot of fun, especially when I had to figure out how to bypass CAPTCHAs after logging in with the script!
