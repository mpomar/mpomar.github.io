About

With years of experience in business consulting, I have had the opportunity to work on complex projects, enhancing my technical expertise while closely collaborating with business stakeholders on processes and strategies. Professionally, I consider myself as a versatile professional with a blend of technical and business skills, possessing equal competence in both domains. This enables me to bridge the gap between business stakeholders and development teams effectively.


My experience primarily revolves around the Pharma & Life Sciences industry, although it is not limited to it. I possess domain knowledge in Salesforce, Veeva, IQVIA, and AWS and, throughout my career, I have successfully delivered projects following both Agile and Waterfall methodologies.


Outside of work, you'll often find me exploring the marvels of nature: being based in Berlin, I have access to the forests and lakes of Brandenburg. When I'm not immersed in nature, you can usually find me in front of my computer, engaging in content creation or programming for personal projects. ChatGPT To Twitch
AI Chatbot For Twitch

As AI is a widely discussed topic today and I have a passion for live streaming, I combined these two realms by creating a Twitch chatbot that allows users to send their chat inputs to ChatGPT and receive responses directly within the chat. A seamless solution to enhance user engagement during live streams.

Tennis Analysis

Being an enthusiastic tennis fan, I have developed a script that simplifies the analysis of tennis matches by providing a comprehensive comparison of key statistics between two opponents. It showcases the potential of leveraging statistics in tennis, where an abundance of data is available.

Strava To Telegram
Telegram Bot

Strava to Telegram is a script that connects your activities' data from Strava to a Telegram bot, allowing you to access your Strava statistics conveniently within the popular messaging app. The script can be easily expanded to pull other data tables from Strava.
